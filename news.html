About Frank:

Aside from wanderlust and python, he keeps deep and high interest in singing, song writing, cooking, playing and learning musical instruments, reading, anime, drawing and cultures around the world! His favorite characters in Harry Potter is Snape and Dumbledore. His aim in life is to learn as much skill as possible and his dream is to go live in the wild with nature!
