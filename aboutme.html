About Frank:

Frank is a traveller, adventurer and a happy go lucky person who believes in simple living and high thinking. Besides traveling and wandering Frank loves to code and he especially loves Python. He believes in doing stuff with his own hands and values hardwork and dedication more than anything. He also believes that love is the most powerful thing in the world. Aside from wanderlust and python, he keeps deep and high interest in singing, song writing, cooking, playing and learning musical instruments, reading, anime, drawing and cultures around the world! His favorite characters in Harry Potter is Snape and Dumbledore. His aim in life is to learn as much skill as possible and his dream is to go live in the wild with nature!

Who is Frank?

Frank Brano Gomes is a student of life. He is currently in quarantine in Kolkata, India and he is aiming to learn as much skill as he can! He chooses a project and works to see it off. He is unfazed by challenges that comes in his way and focuses on the solution part rather than the problem. He is not very active in social media but keeps track of the digital world.

What is the aim of this site?

The objectives of this site is two fold. First, it objective is to help Frank keep record of his learning progress. He will be writing about everything he has learnt so far in his journey and hopefully share his knowledge and his talents with the world. And Second, he wants to complete his ultimate goal!

What is Frank's ultimate goal?

This Covid-19 pandemic has taught Frank a valuable lesson, "Life is short and filled with uncertainty, so instead of entertaining the thoughts on what might happen, we can truly focus on living life to the fullest, living each day with happiness." So Frank wants to prove the world that whatever might happen along the way if you focus your energy on positivity you can live a happy, lively life and make each day the happiest day of your life! That is his ultimate goal!
